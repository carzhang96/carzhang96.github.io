Photographs by Juan Osorio Cardboard Chair Design


GROUP PROJECT


Collaborators: Sarah Ibrahim
Size: 22 x 30 in panel (left), 45 x 45 x 92 cm model (right)
Medium: Pencil on paper panel; Cardboard chair model
Description:

With this project we were challenged to design a functional cardboard chair using only one and a half 1.5 x 2 m cardboard sheets. Our design derives from trying to use as few pieces as possible. Thus the chair elegantly folds out from one large flat piece of cardboard with only a couple other pieces for support.
