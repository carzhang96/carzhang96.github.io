GROUP PROJECT


Collaborators: Sarah Ibrahim (left panel)
Size: 22 x 30 in. panels (top), 1:20 scale model (bottom)
Medium: Pencil on paper panel; Chipboard, foamboard, and wood model
Description:

A proposal for new stairs in a unit of Le Corbusier's Unit√© D'Habitation. Our design focuses on maximizing the functionality of the stairs. Thus, the side of the staircase is composed of small cubby-like spaces and the underside of the staircase is also left open, like shelves, for storage. The rectangular compartments are designed using Le Corbusier's Modular dimensions and they contain a red, blue, or yellow backing to recognize Le Corbusier's key design elements and fit into the enviornment of the building.
