Casa Malaparte


GROUP PROJECT


Collaborators: Alan Chu, Jessica Chen
Size: 70 x 70 x 34 cm
Medium: Chipboard
Description:

A contour model of Casa Malaparte, a house in Punta Massullo on the Isle of Capri designed by architects Adalberto Libera and Curzio Malaparte. This project was an analysis of topothesia, a building's integration into its landscape and surroundings.


Casa Malaparte, located on a 32 meter high cliff overlooking the Gulf of Salerno, is well integrated into its environment as it emerges from the surrounding landscape and is built with local stone extracted from the site itself.
